Engr. Ime Obot Etuk

He was a kind man. Engr. Ime who was fondly called mark by his friends,
was a peace-loving and sociable person. He hated to see situations where the strong oppressed the weak or where people were abused.

Engr. Ime Isaiah Obot Etuk hated lies with passion. He was a man of honour, principle and dignity. His love was unconditional. He was bold, brave and and full of energy. Laziness was never part of who he was. Ever ready and ever active man. Very intelligent and sweet to be with.

Because of this man, many people will always have food on their table. He dedicated his time and energy training several other young men to be as good and even better than he was in the field of marine engineering. He saved lives through his kind gestures. He is a true hero of our time.

In the city of Port-harcourt, he met and worked with several foreign engineers in the oil and gas companies. Most of whom had recommended him to other companies that needed such expatriate. He treated strangers like family with no form of discrimination. They always called him 'brother' due to how he loved and protected them.

A man that worried so much about the welfare of others. A true leader and friends. He was like a father, a bother and a friend to those who knew him well. He loved God and the things of God with all his might. Whenever there was a little time from his duties, the house of God his dwelling place. He respected people whom God has called to lead. He was always ready to serve.

This 'hero' will be greatly missed.

- Dearest Uncle
